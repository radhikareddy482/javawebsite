Naveen Babu Ghanta,better known by his nickname Nani,is an indian actor who stars predominantly inTelugu films and a few Tamil films.
After his educatin,he became an assistant director and worked with srinu vaitla and Bapu,before working as an RJfor world space satillite in Hyderabad.
He won the state Nandi Award for Best Actor for "Yeto Vellipoindhi Manasu",and the Filmfare Critics Award for Best Actor -South for "Bhale Bhale Magadivoy
