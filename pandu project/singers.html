Sonu Nigam is an Indian playback singer whose songs have been featured mainly in Hindi and Kannada.
He has also sung in Bengali,Gujarati,Tamil,Telugu,Marathi,Tulu,Assamese,Odia.


Muhammad Atif Aslam is a Pakistani singer-songwriter and actor.His movie debut was the 2011 social drama Bol.
He has recorded numerous chart-topping songs and is known for his vocal belting technique.
